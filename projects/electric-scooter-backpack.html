Electric Scooter Backpack

This is a personal experiment in shrinking an electric scooter down into something you can wear. The idea is a last‑mile vehicle that lives in a backpack: you carry it onto the bus or into class, then unfold it and ride home.

I’m still in the early stages of packaging and safety design, so this page is mostly a placeholder for now. The plan is to fit the drivetrain, battery pack, and folding frame into a backpack‑sized footprint while keeping the center of mass low enough to feel stable when riding.

More details, sketches, and prototypes coming soon.
