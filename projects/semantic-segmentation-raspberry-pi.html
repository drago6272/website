Semantic Segmentation on Raspberry Pi

This was my final project for 18-794: Introduction to Deep Learning and Pattern Recognition for Computer Vision. My group built an on-edge segmentation model that runs on a Raspberry Pi 4 (4GB) to classify groceries. Despite the limited hardware, we got the model running at around 8 fps with decent mIoU and AP.
